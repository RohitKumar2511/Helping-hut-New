About The NGO

Helping Hut envisions a society free from beggary, where every individual in need enjoys basic living rights. Our mission is to create employment opportunities for the underprivileged, eliminate begging, and drive positive change through online campaigns, on-ground efforts, and empowerment initiatives. Together, we aim to bring joy, awareness, and support to those in need, uplifting lives from deprivation to knowledge and resources, and spreading light across communities.

How We are making the difference

Transforming Lives with Compassionate Impact

At our NGO, we believe in the power of compassion to create lasting change. Every initiative we undertake is driven by a deep commitment to uplift and support communities, empowering individuals to overcome challenges and reach their full potential.

Empowering Campaigns by Helping Hut NGO

  • EDUCATION AWARENESS CAMPAIGN : The higher the level of education, the less likely the person is to fall below the poverty line. This is because education imparts skills and knowledge associated with higher wages. Educating girls in particular makes them more likely to take control of decisions relating to fertility, family welfare, and health.
  • HEALTH AWARENESS CAMPAIGN : On the occasion of teacher's day with the cooperation of coordinators of Helping Hut in collaboration with Mr. Pramod Kumar, Assistant Professor at University of Delhi, conducted an awareness session on "Importance of studies on children's life and being independent especially for rural children ''.
  • HELPING VOICELESS : We see Many homeless stray animals panting in summers and shivering in winters. Many of them becomes emaciated due to Starvation. Just to make those sufferings of theirs a little less, please join hands and help us.

    • Ground work at Helping Hut brings smiles to the needy through constant efforts, spreading awareness, and distributing masks, education kits, and clothes. We also empower urban and rural children with education, helping them soar to great heights.
